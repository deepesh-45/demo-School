About Our Institution

Providing high-standard English education, modern infrastructure, and fostering potential for 25 years.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of educational excellence, fostering inquisitive, knowledgeable, and compassionate global citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow with confidence and integrity.

Our Mission

To provide a robust, holistic educational foundation that inspires students to achieve academic mastery, develop technological and athletic abilities, and cultivate ethical values that benefit society as a whole.

Academic Structure

Curriculum & Programs

We offer standard, rigorous academic courses certified for primary, secondary, and higher secondary excellence.

Primary Education

Focusing on early literacy, elementary math, science explorations, interactive arts, and basic social skills, establishing a strong foundation.

Classes I - V

Secondary Education

Core disciplines including mathematics, physics, chemistry, languages, history, and physical education. Focusing on analytical problem-solving.

Classes VI - X

Higher Secondary

Specialized streams (Science: PCM/PCB, Commerce, Humanities) designed to build domain mastery and prepare students for university admissions.

Classes XI - XII

STEM & Innovation

Advanced science programs, software classes, robotics design, coding algorithms, and hands-on tinkering labs to foster inventive thinking.

All Grades

Arts & Culture

Integrating theater arts, dance choreography, classical music training, painting, and public speaking programs to nurture creative talent.

MEMS Journey

25 Years of Academic Excellence

Trace the growth milestones of Mahankal English Medium School from inception to the digital age.

2001

The Foundation Laid

MEMS was founded in Siya, Dewas with the vision of providing high-quality English medium education. Started with 150 primary pupils.

2010

High School Expansion

Upgraded with state-recognized secondary certification. Introduced chemistry/physics science laboratories and sports coaching camps.

2018

Higher Secondary Streams

Opened streams for Class XI and XII (Science and Commerce streams). Built advanced library catalogs and secondary science laboratories.

2026

Digital & Innovation Leap

Celebrated Silver Jubilee! Integrated robotics innovation centers, tablet-based digital learning, and smart classroom boards for all grades.
